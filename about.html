The Stories

When doubt clouds your path in doing God's work, You shouldn't be Afraid. Cause He is with you all the Time.

Michael, Founder of ChurchViz

It all started in 2022. I was just lying in bed, scrolling through Instagram when I came across a post that caught my eye. It was a Bible verse, but what really got me interested were the designs. They were so well-made, and it got me thinking about doing something similar. But, in the end, I didn't pursue it because the idea didn't really grab me.

In 2023, I finally felt certain that it was time to begin this project. It all started when I joined my church's multimedia team. As Christmas drew nearer, the church began organizing its celebrations. What really caught my attention was when I was asked to direct the visuals at my youth Christmas events. It reminded me of the idea I had back in 2022. Suddenly, I found myself unable to resist the urge to start building this community.

After much research and planning, I finally took the first step on my project, initially named "ChurchVisual." I was excited to see how it would turn out. The outcome was unbelievably supportive. Some of my friends also wanted to get involved, seeing it as a great opportunity to showcase their talents in creating, designing, and innovating visual resources for churches. All of this was driven by our intention, only for the Glory of God.

As time passed, "ChurchVisual" became "ChurchViz," and the rest is history.
